About Us

Origin Story

Many years ago, my grandfather was looking for a restaurant in Charlotte that reminded him of home, that gave him the same comfort food he remembered from when he was a child. After meeting my grandmother, he decided to stop looking and open up H-Thai-ML. We continue to serve this community with the same love and respect my grandparents did and hope you enjoy your experience every visit!
